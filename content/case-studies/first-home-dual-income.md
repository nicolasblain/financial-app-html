## Situation

Dual-income couple, mid-30s, 90k combined gross. Saving 10k/year into TFSAs. Considering a 550k condo with 80k saved for down payment.

## Plan

Scenario modeling showed the purchase at asking price would cut retirement contributions by 60% for the first five years — pushing their projected retirement age from 64 to 71. A reduced-scope purchase (480k) kept the retirement date inside their stated goal window.

## Outcome

They revised their search range and closed on a smaller property four months later. Retirement contributions resumed at their previous rate by year two.
