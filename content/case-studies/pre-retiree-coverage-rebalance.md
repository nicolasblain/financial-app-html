## Situation

Single, no dependants, mortgage-free, age 58. Held a 500k term life policy from twenty years earlier. Annual premium: 2,400.

## Plan

The protection review flagged that the original reason for the policy (young children, mortgage) no longer applied. Critical illness coverage was recommended instead, sized to cover a year of expenses and uncovered treatments.

## Outcome

Life policy terminated. CI policy at 100k put in place for roughly half the previous premium. Difference redirected to TFSA.
