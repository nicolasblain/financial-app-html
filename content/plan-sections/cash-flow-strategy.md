## Context

The cash flow section anchors the rest of the plan. Every recommendation elsewhere is limited by what the client can actually commit to save each month.

## Recommendations

- Target savings rate: specified per client, but rarely below 15% of gross income when retirement is more than 10 years away.
- Emergency fund: 3 to 6 months of essential expenses, held in HISA or cashable GIC.

## Rationale

Clients underestimate fixed expenses and overestimate discretionary ones. A measured baseline prevents the plan from assuming savings the client cannot produce.
