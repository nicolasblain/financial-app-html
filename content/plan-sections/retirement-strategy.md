## Context

Bridges accumulation (while working) and decumulation (in retirement). Both phases must reference the same goal ranking captured during goal setting.

## Recommendations

- Account priority order: TFSA → RRSP → non-registered, adjusted for marginal rate.
- Drawdown order: non-registered → RRSP/RRIF → TFSA, with tax-bracket management.

## Rationale

Order of withdrawals affects lifetime tax paid more than any single-year decision. Lock in the framework once; revisit annually.
