## Context

Housing is usually the largest line item in the plan. It deserves its own section even when no purchase is imminent, because the rent-or-buy question is rarely about the numbers alone.

## Recommendations

- Keep all-in housing costs under the ratio specified in the cash flow section.
- If buying, run the scenario under a stress rate 2 points above the current one.

## Rationale

Locks in housing decisions against the rest of the plan rather than against what the bank will lend.
