## Context

The risk management section documents what the client is protected against and what is left uncovered on purpose.

## Recommendations

- Review group benefits annually (they change).
- Individual coverage sized to income replacement plus known obligations.

## Rationale

Explicit "uncovered on purpose" notes prevent future second-guessing during life events.
