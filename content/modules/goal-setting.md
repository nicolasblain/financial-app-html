## Purpose

Move beyond the generic "retirement at 65" checklist into a real statement of what the client wants the money to do. The output becomes the reference point every later recommendation is measured against.

## Inputs

- Life priorities (1, 5, 10, 20-year horizons)
- Financial anxieties and what would relieve them
- Non-negotiables vs. nice-to-haves

## Steps

1. Client completes the reflection prompts in the portal.
2. Planner reviews responses before the workshop.
3. During the workshop, priorities are ranked and confirmed in writing.

## Outputs

- Ranked goal list, re-used throughout the plan and monitoring phases.
