## Purpose

Prevent the plan from becoming a static document. Each year, the client revisits goals, updates data, and the plan is refreshed based on what actually happened.

## Inputs

- Updated income and asset snapshot
- Life events logged during the year
- Outstanding recommendations from last year's plan

## Steps

1. Client updates intake data.
2. Planner runs the delta report.
3. Review session focuses on what changed and what to adjust.

## Outputs

- Updated plan sections.
- New recommendations queue for the next year.
