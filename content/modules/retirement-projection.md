## Purpose

Turn the client's current savings rate into a realistic projection of what retirement will look like, including sensitivity to the assumptions most likely to be wrong.

## Inputs

- Current assets by account type (RRSP, TFSA, non-registered)
- Annual contributions
- Target retirement age and desired lifestyle
- Expected government benefits (CPP/QPP, OAS)

## Steps

1. Build a base-case projection using the client's current savings rate.
2. Stress-test with lower returns and a shorter working horizon.
3. Identify the lever with the highest impact on the gap (savings rate, retirement age, or spending).

## Outputs

- Retirement gap number and the single highest-leverage action.
- Linked plan section: Retirement Strategy.
