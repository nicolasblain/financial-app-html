## Purpose

Build the complete picture of the client's earned income before the first working session. Covers salaried employees, self-employed, and mixed situations.

## Inputs

- Employment status (employee, self-employed, retired, mixed)
- Base compensation and pay cadence
- Variable compensation (bonus, commission, stock)
- Group benefits booklet (if employee)
- Last two years of T4 / T4A / T2125

## Steps

1. Client fills the employment section of the intake form.
2. Client uploads supporting documents.
3. Planner reviews completeness and flags missing items.

## Outputs

- Normalized annual income used by the cash flow analysis module.
- Group benefits summary used by the protection module.
