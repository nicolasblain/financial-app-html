## Purpose

Most clients are either over-insured on the wrong product or under-insured on the right one. This module separates existing coverage from actual need and flags the gap.

## Inputs

- Group benefits booklet
- Individual policies (life, disability, CI)
- Dependants and obligations

## Steps

1. Catalog every existing policy with coverage amount, cost, and term.
2. Calculate the need (income replacement + obligations).
3. Compare and recommend specific adds, drops, or replacements.

## Outputs

- Coverage gap analysis.
- Linked plan section: Risk Management.
