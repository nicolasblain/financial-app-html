## Purpose

Frame the home purchase question in terms of the client's full plan rather than a standalone affordability number. Connects to cash flow, retirement savings rate, and emergency fund targets.

## Inputs

- Target price range and down payment saved
- Household net income and fixed expenses
- Current rent and expected ownership costs (taxes, insurance, maintenance)
- Existing debts and credit score

## Steps

1. Establish the realistic price band from the client's actual savings rate, not the bank's maximum.
2. Model the impact on retirement savings under each scenario.
3. Flag tradeoffs explicitly — what plan goal has to bend if this purchase goes ahead?

## Outputs

- Go / wait / reduce-scope recommendation with rationale.
- Linked plan section: Housing Strategy.
